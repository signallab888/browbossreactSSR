  ---
  Aftercare
           
  The first 24 hours are the most important. Keep your lashes completely dry. No water, no steam, no sweating, no mascara. Sleep on your back if you
  can.                                                                                                                                                  
   
  For the first 48 hours, handle them gently. No rubbing, no pulling, no eyelash curler.                                                                
                  
  After that, use a non-oily makeup remover and avoid anything heavy around the eye area. After the first week, you can condition your lashes with a    
  small amount of castor or coconut oil to keep them healthy between appointments.
                                                                                                                                                        
  Wait at least 8 weeks before your next lift. Going sooner risks over-processing, which is the main cause of lash damage after this treatment.

  FAQ
     
  Does a lash lift damage your natural lashes?
  No, not when it is done correctly. The main causes of damage are leaving the solution on too long and going back too soon. YUMI's keratin formula     
  nourishes while it lifts, so you leave with lashes in better shape than when you came in. We are certified and trained to time every step properly.   
                                                                                                                                                        
  Does it hurt?                                                                                                                                         
  No. Most clients find it relaxing and some fall asleep. You may feel slight pressure from the silicone pad but there is no pain. The whole treatment
  is non-invasive from start to finish.                                                                                                                 
   
  How long do results last?                                                                                                                             
  Most clients see a full lift for 8 to 10 weeks. Some go up to 12. The lift does not fall out. It grows out naturally with your lash cycle, so it fades
   gradually rather than all at once.                                                                                                                   
   
  Is tint included?                                                                                                                                     
  Tint is available as an add-on during the same appointment. Most clients include it because it is the step that makes mascara completely unnecessary.
  Just let us know when you book.                                                                                                                       
                  
  Can I wear mascara after?                                                                                                                             
  Wait 24 hours, then water-based mascara is fine. Most clients find they do not need it anymore. If you want the darkest possible result, adding a tint
   to your appointment is the better option.                                                                                                            
   
  What is the difference between a lash lift and extensions?                                                                                            
  Extensions add synthetic fibers to your natural lashes using adhesive and need fills every 2 to 3 weeks to stay looking full. A lash lift works only
  with your own lashes. No glue, no fibers, no maintenance appointments. You book once and come back when the cycle is done.                            
                  
  I have short lashes. Will it still work?                                                                                                              
  It depends on the actual length. Very short lashes may not give you a dramatic result. We assess before we start and we will not go through with it if
   it is not going to do anything noticeable for you. Most clients with short but present lashes see a real difference.                                 
   
  How soon can I book again?                                                                                                                            
  Wait at least 8 weeks. This gives the previous lift time to grow out fully and keeps your lashes from being over-processed.
                                                                                                                                                        
  What do I need to do before my appointment?
  Come with clean lashes. No mascara, no eyelash curler in the 24 hours before. Remove contact lenses before we start. That is it.                      
                                                                                                                                                        
  Is it safe during pregnancy?
  The treatment is non-invasive and considered safe during pregnancy. If you are in your first trimester or have specific concerns, check with your     
  doctor first. We are happy to answer any questions when you call.                                                                                     
   
  What if I do not like the result?                                                                                                                     
  The lift cannot be reversed, so we take the consultation seriously. If you are nervous about going too dramatic, tell us before we start. We will
  choose a subtle rod and a natural angle. Our goal is always to enhance what is already there, not overdo it.
